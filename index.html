About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you fight against endless waves of monsters. Upgrade your character and unlock new abilities to survive as long as possible.

Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys to move. Attacks are automatic. Collect experience orbs to level up and choose new abilities.

Features

  • Endless waves of unique monsters
  • Multiple character classes
  • RPG-style progression system
  • Beautiful pixel art graphics

Tips

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded
  • Prioritize area damage abilities
  • Collect health pickups when available
  • Upgrade your favorite abilities first
